Mimosa Fruit Salad

mimosa
Ingredients
  • 2 cups halved clementine segments
  • 2 cups sliced halved peeled kiwi
  • 2 cups diced watermelon, cantaloupe or honeydew melon
  • 1 cup halved blackberries
  • 1 cup quartered strawberries
  • ½ cup orange juice
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh mint
Preparation
  1. Combine clementines, kiwi, melon, blackberries and strawberries in a large bowl. Stir prosecco, orange juice and mint together in a small bowl. Pour over the fruit and stir gently.
Nutrition Information
  • Per serving: 90 calories; 0 g fat(0 g sat); 3 g fiber; 17 g carbohydrates; 1 g protein; 31 mcg folate; 0
  • cholesterol; 11 g sugars; 0 g added sugars; 366 IU vitamin A; 72 mg vitamin C; 36 mg calcium; 1 mg iron; 3mg sodium; 296 mg potassium
  • Nutrition Bonus: Vitamin C (120% daily value)
  • Carbohydrate Servings: 1
  • Exchanges: 1 fruit, ½ alcohol
