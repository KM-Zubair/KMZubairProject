Smoky Grilled Flank Steak

avocado
Ingredients
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1½ teaspoons distilled white vinegar
  • 1½ teaspoons smoked paprika
  • ½ teaspoon ground chipotle chile
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground pepper
  • 1 pound flank steak, trimmed
Preparation
  1. Preheat grill to medium-high.
  2. Combine oil, vinegar, paprika, brown sugar, chipotle, salt, garlic powder and pepper in a small bowl. Rub all over steak. Grill until an instant-read thermometer inserted in the thickest part registers 120°F for medium-rare, 3 to 5 minutes per side. Let rest for 5 minutes. Slice against the grain.
Nutrition Information
  • 211 calories; 12 g fat(4 g sat); 0 g fiber; 2 g carbohydrates; 23 g protein; 8 mcg folate; 68 mg cholesterol; 1 g sugars; 1 g added sugars; 525 IU vitamin A; 0 mg vitamin C; 20 mg calcium; 2 mg iron; 346 mg sodium; 306 mg potassium
  • Serving size: 3.5 ounces
  • Nutrition Bonus:Folate (44% daily value), Vitamin C (40% dv)
  • Carbohydrate Servings: 1½
  • Exchanges:3½ lean protein, 1 fat
