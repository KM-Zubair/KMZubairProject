Introduction

This project is developed to help people of all walks of life, we perform watchful element determination to exploit the data reflected in the system users reviews. Furthermore, since getting food is frequently the very most basic need, we generated recommendations, processes to get instant food not just for an individual person, but also for a group of internet users. This system needs to consider the usability of all individuals in a group and offer service that satisfies the group of users according to some criterion. We made this website to allow people to make food without having to buy expensive dishes and also helping people to control their diet.
