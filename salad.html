Egg Salad Avocado Toast

avocado
Ingredients
  • 8 large eggs
  • ¼ teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon raspberry jam
  • 1 tablespoon slivered almonds
  • Hass Avocados
  • 1 slice whole-wheat toast
Preparation
  1. Mash avocado with celery, lemon juice, hot sauce and salt in a small bowl. Mix in hard-boiled egg. Spread on toast.
  2. Peel the eggs and place 2 in each of 4 serving bowls; sprinkle evenly with salt. To make ahead: Hold eggs in the pot at 140°F for up to 2 hours. Or cool them in an ice-water bath for 10 minutes and refrigerate in an airtight container for up to 1 week. Reheat at 140°F for 15 minutes before serving.
Nutrition Information
  • Serving size: 2 toast
  • Per serving: 143 calories; 10 g fat(3 g sat); 0 fiber; 1 g carbohydrates; 13 g protein; 47 mcg folate; 372 mg cholesterol; 0 g sugars; 0 g added sugars; 540 IU vitamin A; 0 vitamin C; 56 mg calcium; 2 mg iron; 262 mg sodium; 138 mg potassium
  • Nutrition Bonus:Nutrition Bonus: Vitamin A (195% daily value), Vitamin C (32% dv)
  • Nutrition Bonus: Calcium (48% daily value), Folate (24% dv)
  • Carbohydrate Servings: 2½
  • Exchanges: 1½ fat, 1 medium-fat protein, 1 starch
